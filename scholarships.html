The Chancellor's Vanguard Award

Merit-Based All Majors Portfolio Reviewed

Our most prestigious award. Granted to only 5 incoming students each year who demonstrate raw, undeniable, generation-defining talent in their submitted portfolios. This grant covers full undergraduate tuition, housing, and provides a $5,000 annual material stipend for art supplies and software.

Up to $15,000 / yr

Global Perspectives Grant

Need & Merit International Students

Specifically structured for our international applicants. Downtown University College thrives exactly because of our diverse, global student body. This massive grant significantly lowers out-of-country tuition rates, making our Philadelphia campus financially accessible to artists worldwide.

Up to $8,000 / yr

The Creative Tech Innovator Fellowship

Merit-Based Tech & Animation Majors

Aimed at cutting-edge tech majors—including UI/UX designers, 3D animators, and creative coders. Supported directly by prominent Bay Area alumni, this fellowship guarantees you priority access to heavy-render server farms and the latest VR headsets.
